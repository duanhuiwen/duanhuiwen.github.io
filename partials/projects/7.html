Smart Space for Personal Guidance

2013.3 - 2013.4 | Espoo, Finland

Key words: indoor navigation

Spagu project studies indoor navigation technologies and solutions for the museum environment. The aim is to study the hardware and application-level techniques for users to monitor and guide, as well as a better understanding of users' spatial perception. Indoor navigation is based on the user's identifier(tag), the user's routes will be sent to remote server for further analysis.


Project goals:

  • Examine the Youtube CDN from 4-5 different locations: university, home and three Amazon EC2 instances (Ireland, Singapore, California). For each geographical location, you will identify the content servers IP address corresponding to a certain number of predifined Youtube links (20).
  • Upload videos from different locations, observe how a new content gets populated over the CDN

Tools and technology:

  • Pytomo for crawling the destination IP information.
  • Amazon EC2.
